# Code of conduct

PulsePlayer welcomes respectful, technically rigorous collaboration.

Contributors and maintainers must:

- communicate without harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks;
- critique ideas and code, not people;
- respect privacy and never publish credentials or private media endpoints;
- accept maintainer decisions on scope, safety, and release readiness;
- make space for participants of different experience levels and backgrounds.

Unacceptable behavior may result in edited or removed content, a warning,
temporary restriction, or a permanent ban. Report conduct concerns privately
through the repository maintainer's GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed
fairly and kept confidential to the extent practical.

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and other
project spaces.
